I perform regularly on piano, organ, and keyboards. I enjoy playing a wide range of music from Bach and Brahms to gospel, R&B, and jazz. I have composed numerous original works and arrangements for choir, piano, strings, and other instruments, including congregational hymns and worship songs. I have served as music and worship director at churches in New York, Indiana, and Illinois of several denominations and worship styles.

I am available for church services, weddings, funerals, and other events. Please reach out for more information.
