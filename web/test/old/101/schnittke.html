Alfred Schnittke (1934-1998)

By Andrew Cashner

Music 101, University of Chicago, Spring 2014, Professor Cashner

Profile

Alfred Schnittke experimented in every style of modern music during the last years of the Soviet Union. He pioneered a technique and aesthetic of "polystylism," and is considered a pioneer of musical postmodernism.
