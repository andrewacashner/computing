Join me for Quilisma, a weekly half-hour of singing Gregorian chant for enjoyment. I will be leading this group at the University of Chicago, starting in January 2014. Quilisma is open to anyone, regardless of their musical background, who would like to stop in and try something new.

It meets on Thursdays from 12 to 12:30 p.m. on the top landing of the wonderfully resonant Goodspeed Hall stairwell.
