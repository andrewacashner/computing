Hëgi:wajë:tho’

    • I:’ I/me o’gaewayëda:the’t become my duty hëgi:wajë:tho’ to gather the words öëdö:h in front of gaewadehgöh message/address ganö:nyök thanksgiving/gratitude sögwa:wi:h he gave it to us sögwajë:no’kda’öh. our creator
      • It has become my duty to gather the opening words of thanksgiving that our creator gave to us.
    • Ayë: it seems like de’ögwaögëh we haven’t heard it gwisdë’ something ayoiwanä:gwahdö:k shocking news agayë:da’k it will be there ganödagwëhda:’ the village/town.
      • It seems like we haven’t heard much shocking news happening in the village.
    • Ne’hoh nö:nä:h there maybe niyo’dë:h it’s that way gagëödö:nyo’ it's laying about dejoewadiyö:dö’ at death’s door gonökdehshä. sicknesses
      • There might be sicknesses going around, and some people may be about to die.
    • Da:’ o:nëh dih and so now wa’agwadeošök we pray sgë:nö’ wellness dösaenödawënye:’ they will walk around yöëdzada:je’. upon the earth
      • And so now we pray that wellness will spread all around us upon the earth.
    • Da:ne’hoh dih and so then nëyögwa’nigo’dëök. let it be that way in our minds
      • And so then let it be that way in our minds.

Ha’deyögwe’dage:h

    • Hawe:’öh he intended ganöhgwa’shä’ love ëyögwayë:da’k we will have it neh the/it yöëdza’geh on the earth deyögwadawënye:h. we are walking about
      • He intended that we should have love while we are living on the earth.
    • Da:’ onëh dih and so now ëswe:he:k you will all realize it ëdwajë:nö:ni’ we will do our best sga:d one hëdwa:yë:’ we will put it down ögwa’nigöë’ our minds/thoughts dëdwadahnö:nyö:’ we will honor each other ha’deyögwe’dage:h. all kinds of people
      • And so now let us all do our best to make our minds as one as we honor each other for all kinds of people.
    • Da:ne’hoh dih and so then nëyögwa’nigo’dëök. let it be that way in our minds
      • And so then let it be that way in our minds.
